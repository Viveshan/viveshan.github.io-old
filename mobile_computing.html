We will advise on the most suitable handheld or wearable terminal.

Rugged/Industrial handheld terminal: When mobile workers need to collect data in the harshest environments

Light Industrial handheld terminals: When Mobile workers need to collect data in normal working conditions such as retail

PDA/EDA/Enterprise handheld terminals: For mobile workers to collect data, make calls and access other applications.

Multimodal & Standard Wearable Terminals: Offer the user the freedom of having both hands available in the order picking environment alongside a voice or RF Scanning Solution

Vehicle Mounted Terminals: Attached to forklift trucks and other mobile vehicles.

Tablets: Often used in the customer-facing retail environment
